## 📋 Social Media Do's and Don'ts

### For College Students

---

### ✅ Do's

1. **Use respectful language**  
   Maintain a professional and courteous tone when posting, commenting, or engaging with others online.

2. **Verify before sharing**  
   Check the accuracy of information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.

3. **Secure your accounts**  
   Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all social media platforms.

4. **Build a positive digital footprint**  
   Share constructive, thoughtful content that reflects well on you—employers and institutions often review online presence.

5. **Credit original sources**  
   When reposting or sharing content, give proper credit to the original creator or source.

---

### ❌ Don'ts

1. **Avoid oversharing personal information**  
   Do not publicly share sensitive details like your phone number, address, Aadhaar number, or real-time location.

2. **No offensive or discriminatory content**  
   Avoid posting or engaging with content that is hateful, discriminatory, or inappropriate.

3. **Don't engage in aggressive arguments**  
   Avoid heated online debates. Disagree respectfully or disengage when conversations become toxic.

4. **Don't ignore privacy settings**  
   Regularly review and update your privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information.

5. **Don't spread rumors or unverified news**  
   Refrain from sharing unconfirmed information that could cause panic, harm reputations, or mislead others.

---

> 💡 **Remember:** What you post online contributes to your digital footprint—a record that can impact future academic, career, and personal opportunities.
